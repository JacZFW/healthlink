About HealthLink

Connecting the world to world-class medical care in China. Zero service fees. Expert guidance. Transparent pricing.

Our Story

HealthLink was founded with a simple mission: make world-class medical care in China accessible and affordable to patients around the world.

We saw that millions of patients were paying 3–10x more for medical treatments in the US, Canada, and Europe — often waiting months for care. Meanwhile, China has built a world-class healthcare system with 1,876 Grade 3A hospitals, 200+ Da Vinci surgical robots, and medical technology that rivals or exceeds the West.

Our team of medical coordinators, many with bilingual backgrounds, bridges the gap — handling everything from hospital matching and appointment booking to travel logistics and post-treatment follow-up. And we charge zero service fees to patients. Ever.
